Common Genres We Stock

These are the main genres you’ll find at E.M.C.S Bookshop. This page uses lists to keep the information clear.

  • Fiction: realistic stories about everyday life, friendships, and challenges.
  • Sci‑Fi: space, technology, future worlds, and big ideas.
  • Romance: love stories, relationships, and light reads.
  • Academic: study skills and introductory textbooks.
  • History: people, events, and time periods explained in an easy way.
  • Mystery: clues, secrets, and twists — good if you like solving puzzles.

Quick Picking Tips

  • If you want a fast read, choose shorter chapters or short stories
  • If you want to learn, try history or academic titles
  • If you want pure escapism, sci‑fi is a great starting point
